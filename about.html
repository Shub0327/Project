Character of the School

This school is recognised by the Education Department, Government of Maharashtra.The School is open for all the communities in the society, while presenting the academic excellence with Indian Tradional Cultural Ethos as a hallmark of the school.We conduct classes from Nursery to Std 10th and also prepare students for various competitive exams.

The role of school is to build and develop qualitites of good leadership while enculcating moral values for better tomorrow.

Mission

M

New Blossoms provides your child a platform where values are nurtured with quality,academic excellence and other fun filled activities to develop their life skills. We at New Blossoms, endeavour to inspire and empower our students to be life long learners,critical thinkers,risk takers and competent enough to meet the challenges of ever changing global society.

Vision

V

The vision of New Blossoms taken as an academic discipline in constructivism,enquiry based reflective collaborative and integrative in the study of how knowledge and skills are exchanged in educational context and it considers the interactions that take place during learning.

History

H

Since the establishment in the year 2009 the school has been achieving greater heights with a quest for excellence.Being in rural area it has nurtured students thorugh activity based learning,multiple intelligence,play-way methods etc.The school has made every efforts to educate students and make them the best socially developed.
